Express Hotel & Events is Nyandarua's premier all-in-one hospitality destination — combining a vibrant hotel and lounge with a full-service events and catering operation.

Founded over 14 years ago with a passion for exceptional service, we have grown into one of Kenya's most trusted event and hospitality brands — from intimate private parties to state-level functions and 1,000-guest weddings.

Our award-winning team brings expertise, creativity and precision to everything we do — whether you are reserving a table in our lounge or trusting us with the catering for your child's graduation.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a table?+
You can reserve a table online through our booking form, call us on +254 718 770 846, or send a WhatsApp message. We confirm within 2 hours.
Is there a minimum spend for group bookings?+
For groups of 10+ we require a small reservation deposit to secure the table. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
What sports matches do you show?+
We show all major football leagues — Premier League, Champions League, La Liga, Serie A and international matches including AFCON and World Cup.
Can I book the lounge for a private event?+
Yes! We offer private hire of the lounge for corporate meetings, birthday celebrations and group events. Contact us for pricing and availability.
How far in advance should I book?+
We recommend 4–8 weeks for large events like weddings, and 2–4 weeks for smaller events. Last-minute bookings are accepted based on availability.
Do you travel outside Nyandarua?+
Yes — we operate nationwide across Kenya. Travel and transport costs are quoted separately based on your event location and logistics.
Do you offer tastings before my event?+
Yes! We offer complimentary tastings for wedding catering packages. For corporate events, a sample tasting can be arranged on request.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer and cash. A 40% deposit confirms your booking, with the balance due on or before the event day.
